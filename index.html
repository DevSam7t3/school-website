about BI

Brantford Institute of Sustainable Education and Skills Development is located in the heart of the historic Bell City currently known as Brantford. The Institute is a beacon of purpose-driven education.

Our mission is to equip the next generation of Canadians with the skills and training required to meet the emerging needs of the local industry and meets the Sustainability goals for a sustainable future. The aim is build a skilled workforce equipped with emerging skills and hence to contribute to regional economic growth and ensure long-term prosperity of the country.
